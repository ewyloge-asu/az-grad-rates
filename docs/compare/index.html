    Data table

    Data from the Arizona Department of Education Accountability Research office. Graduation rate is the percentage of students in a four-year cohort who earned a high school diploma by August 31 of their fourth year. ELA and Math passing rates reflect the share of Grade 11 students scoring at or above the proficient level on the AASA assessment. Tool covers 113 traditional Arizona school districts. Methodology and source files available in the project repository.
