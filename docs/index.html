How Arizona's school districts compare on graduation

Four-year graduation rates for every Arizona school district, cohorts 2010 through 2025. The statewide average shown by default; add districts to see how they stack up.

    Showing

    Suppressed values (small cohorts) appear as gaps in the line. Source: Arizona Department of Education, 4-Year Cohort Graduation Rate data, cohorts 2010–2025.

    Data table

    Data from the Arizona Department of Education Accountability Research office. Graduation rate is the percentage of students in a four-year cohort who earned a high school diploma by August 31 of their fourth year. Methodology and source files available in the project repository.
